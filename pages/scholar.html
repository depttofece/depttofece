Scholarship

Some Important Points

  • Last Date Of Online Submittion is 30th Nov, 2021
  • Income Certificate must be of candidate's Gaurdian.
  • The applicant must be a resident of Uttar Pradesh and his/her domicile Certificate must also be of Uttar Pradesh.
  • Candidate's account must be in home branch of university.
  • All the documents must be self attested by the candidates.

Required Documents

  • Income Certificate (Father's Name)
  • Domicile Certificate (Uttar Pradesh)
  • Caste Certificate, if any
  • Online verification slip of Income, Domicile & Caste Certificate
  • College Fee Reciept
  • UPTU Fee Reciept, if any (20,000)
  • Bank Passbook (Verified by Bank Officers)
  • Students College ID Card (Verified by HOD)
  • High School Marksheet
  • Intermediate Marksheet
  • Gap Certificate, if any
  • Affidavit (Candidate)
  • Affidavit (Gaurdian)
  • Aadhar Card
  • Parent's Aadhar Card (Sometimes Required)
