ABOUT BUNDELKHAND UNIVERSITY

The Institute was incepted with introduction of B.Tech. Food Technology course in Academic Session 2000-2001 B.Tech. in six more streams was introduced w.e.f. Academic Year 2002-2003 Intake in each of the seven branch is of 60 students All the courses are approved by AICTE.

VISSION

  • Set the standards for skill and engineering education.
  • Lead all students at BU to experience personal transformation who in turn become agents of community transformation.
  • Become a leading partner in the sustainable growth of the country and the industry.

Mission

  • Institute of Engineering & Technology is committed to serve the students by sensitively addressing their needs through strategically designed administrative procedures
  • Achieves the target of academic excellence, high competency and all round education.
  • To accord dignity to each and every skill by emphasizing its teaching and learning
