ABOUT UNIVERSITY

This seat of higher learning came into existence on August 26, 1975, vide Government of Uttar Pradesh Notification No. 10/15-60/74 under the provision of the U.P. Universities Act.However, though belonging to the younger generation of the Indian Universities, the Bundelkhand University has fast transformed into a University of excellence offering opportunities to the aspiring youths to pursue higher education. However, though belonging to the younger generation of the Indian Universities, applied research and Professional training according to their aptitude and potential.

Welcome to the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) at the IET, BU, Jhansi. The discipline of electronics engineering pervades every aspect of modern society, and continues to be the cornerstone of rapid technological advances that improve the quality of life in this millennium. IET ECE strives to be at the forefront of engineering education to equip our students to be engineering leaders in industry, research, and entrepreneurship.
